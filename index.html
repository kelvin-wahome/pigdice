Welcome to The Pig Dice Game!!

RULES!

  1. Register names for two players
  2. Each player gets a turn to roll the dice
  3. A roll of 2,3,4,5,6 earns the player the respective points
  4. If you choose to hold then the points earned will be added then the next player gets to play their turn
  5. The first player to reach 100 points wins the game

PS!Know when to stop rolling.ALL THE BEST!
